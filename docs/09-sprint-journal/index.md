# Sprint Journal

> The Sprint Journal is the engineering record of the company. What was planned, what was delivered, what was deferred, what was learned. Not a retrospective ceremony — an engineering record.

---

## Purpose of this section

Sprint notes provide the human context behind ADRs and the Decision Log. They capture the technical narrative of how the platform evolved. A new engineer reading the Sprint Journal understands not just what was built, but why it was built in that order, and what was discovered along the way.

## Sprint note format

Each sprint note includes:
- **What was planned**
- **What was delivered**
- **What was deferred** (and why)
- **Key decisions made** (with links to ADRs or Decision Log)
- **What was learned**
