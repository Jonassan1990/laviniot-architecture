# Product

## The core product insight

Industrial IoT has a connectivity problem, not an analytics problem. The hardest work is integrating with PLCs, historians, and legacy protocols — not building dashboards.

The product strategy follows from this: **own the integration layer, and everything above it becomes a differentiator.**
