# Mission

## The mission

> **Connect every industrial asset. Surface every relevant signal. Deliver every critical decision — at the right time, to the right operator.**

---

## What this means in practice

**Connect every industrial asset.**
The platform must support the full range of industrial protocols — OPC-UA, Modbus, MQTT, REST — without requiring customers to replace their existing infrastructure. Integration is the product.

**Surface every relevant signal.**
Raw telemetry is not useful. The platform must normalise, contextualise, and filter data so that what reaches the operator is actionable, not noise.

**Deliver every critical decision — at the right time, to the right operator.**
A warning that arrives late is a failure. A warning that goes to the wrong person is a failure. The platform must understand operational roles, escalation paths, and timing.

---

## Who we serve

**Primary user:** The industrial operator — a person who manages assets, interprets operational data, and makes decisions about physical systems. This person is a domain expert, not an analytics expert.

**Secondary user:** The engineer — a person who configures the platform, defines alarm rules, and integrates new assets.

**Not the primary user:** The data scientist, the executive, the IT administrator.

---

## What LavinIoT is not

| Not this | Why |
|---|---|
| A SCADA replacement | LavinIoT integrates with SCADA — it does not replace control systems |
| A general-purpose IoT platform | The domain focus is industrial; consumer IoT is out of scope |
| A dashboard vendor | The dashboard is an interface to data, not the product |
| An AI model marketplace | AI capabilities are embedded for specific operational decisions |
| A reporting tool | Reports are a byproduct; real-time operational intelligence is the goal |
