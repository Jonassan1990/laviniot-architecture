# Sprint Notes

Sprint notes capture what was planned, what was delivered, what was deferred, and what was learned. They provide the human context behind the ADRs and Decision Log entries.

Sprint notes are written at the end of each sprint. They are not retrospectives in the agile ceremony sense — they are engineering records.
