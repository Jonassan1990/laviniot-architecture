## Summary

LavinIoT builds an **AI-native Industrial IoT platform** that connects physical assets to structured intelligence. The platform serves operators — people who manage factories, energy systems, and infrastructure — not data scientists.

The problem: industrial operations generate enormous volumes of sensor data that is siloed, unstructured, delayed, and inaccessible. The consequence: decisions are reactive, maintenance is expensive, failures are surprises.

LavinIoT solves this by providing a modular, open integration layer between the physical asset layer and the analytical layer, with AI inference available at the edge and in the cloud.
